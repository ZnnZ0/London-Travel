What to Expect When Visiting the United Kingdom

  • Immigration procedures are strict in the United Kingdom. Have your documents ready for what you will be asked for.(Your return ticket, Location of hotel, where you will be staying, Travel dates if you have them)
  • Take a water repellent jacket with hood. As in the mountains, the weather can change quickly and easily in a short period of time.
  • Pay as much as possible by card. (※1)
  • If the receipt says "service charge not included", a gratuity is required. You can leave a tip of about 10% of the fee. (※2)
  • Smoking is not permitted in train stations, on platforms, or at bus stops in the United Kingdom. Furthermore, please note that in some areas outdoor drinking is prohibited.
  • British food is notorious for being bad. You should know some British dishes are served with their own salt and pepper or sauces.
  • Restaurants are extremely expensive, so if you want to keep prices low, you should eat at pubs and markets rather than restaurants.

　

※1

Available cards include :

  • American Express (AMEX)
  • MasterCard and Maestro (cards issued in the U.S., Canada and the Netherlands are not accepted)
  • Visa and V PAY (cards issued outside the UK will not be accepted)

And Apple pay is also available.

　

※2

In the UK, pubs and cafes usually do not tip, but restaurants and hotels do.

  • Hotel room service: 50 pence to 1 pound per visit
  • Restaurants: 10-15% of the total amount without tax
  • Cabs: 10% of the total amount

However, according to people living in the UK, there are no occasions to pay tips except for restaurant tips.
